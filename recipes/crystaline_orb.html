Crystaline Orb

Recipes

This orb holds the power of heaven and hell. Hold it aloft at the temple of war to unlock it's secrets.

Ingredients

  • Mysterious Orb
  • 30 Magic Crystals
  • 15 Demon dust
  • 15 Angel dust

Steps

  1. Place Mysterious Orb in Crystal Infusion Chamber
  2. Load Magic Crystals into Crystal slot on Crystal Infusion Chamber
  3. Load Demon dust and Angel dust into dust slot on Crystal Infsion Chamber
  4. Run Crystal Infusion Chamber on mid-high for 30min
