Godslayer Blade

Recipies

A Godslayer Blade is a held weapon

The Godslayer Blade does 2X damage to large and gargantuan enemies

Base elemental damage:Neon Available augments Thunder, Flame

Grants the Wombo Combo status effect when hits 3 criticals in a row

Ingredients

  • 10 Large Enemy Souls
  • 5 Gargantuan Enemy Souls
  • 20 Oulite Bars
  • 5 Neon Energy

Steps

  1. Set the 20 Oulite Bars into the Aeon Forge
  2. When fully smelted, remove slag carefully with slotted slag-ladle
  3. Extract Oulite Sparsing particles with Aeon Particle Net
  4. Pour the molten Oulite into Illuminite Forming Press
  5. Insert all 5 Neon energy into energy deposit chamber on Illuminite Forming Press
